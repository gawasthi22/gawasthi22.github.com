Industries

The influx of new technologies and business models demands a global vision and an in-depth understanding of the client business needs in order to offer flexible and scalable solutions that offer higher RoI. We leverage our know-how of different business verticals with our expertise across various technologies to deliver the most ingenious solutions to our clients.

Media & Entertainment

With the advent of the Internet, the reach of the media is at an all-time high. There is strong focus on how to use the various channels of media to increase awareness about marketer products with the objective of generating more business and improving bottom lines. The unique aspect of applications in the Media & Entertainment (M&E) space is that it helps in not only increasing the brand recognition and brand recall value, but also enhancing productivity and reducing the cost of marketing. Accurate measurability of online marketing also makes it a very compelling proposition.

Travel & Hospitality

In today's fast paced travel & hospitality industry, Pace IT understands the dynamism in its rapid growth, fierce competition and increasing customer expectations. The company has vast experience and an in-depth knowledge of the industry and operates through active offshore engagements with major players spread over in US, UK, Europe & Australia.

Pace IT is your Travel technology partner to help you build business aligned, scalable solutions using latest technologies for this industry (Hospitality, Travel and MICE).

Retail & Distribution

The competitiveness of the Retail industry demands that we develop solutions for our clients that are in tandem with their vision to succeed. The ever-evolving technological landscape presents an array of opportunities to increase an organization's profitability; however, the solutions must implement ideas with flexibility, speed and innovation. Pace IT's solutions help retail operations gain the competitive edge through the ingenious use of Information Technology. Over the years we have gained an in-depth knowledge of the retail industry, which enables us to provide meaningful and cost-effective solutions for your business.

Healthcare & Life Sciences

With changing landscape, the Healthcare and Life sciences organizations are facing a multitude of challenges. These organizations have to reduce errors, improve quality of care, reduce time to market, and comply with constantly evolving regulatory rules and guidelines, while keeping costs of operations low. This has made the entire process very complicated and put an enormous pressure on the Healthcare and Life sciences organizations. The solution to this complicated process lies in Health IT systems. Health IT systems come as rescue for such organizations by helping them in avoiding costly mistakes, improving productivity, and making the operational process more cost effective.

Telecom

Telecom industry has entered into an era of consolidation and changes; developing new products and services in wire-line, wireless, internet, data, voice and mobile are a high priority. Ever demanding end-users with limited budgets, swift technology changes and competitive market conditions are the challenging scenarios faced by the telecom vendors today. Pace IT’s proven experience in maintaining such complex systems and developing and integrating solutions in advanced telecommunications and OSS systems using technologies like C++, CORBA, OSP and CSP can help in providing high quality, cost-effective and on-time solutions.
