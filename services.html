System Integration

System Integration is the process of linking together different computing systems and software applications physically or functionally, to act as a co-ordinated whole. In today’s connected world, the role of system integration engineers is becoming more and more important: more and more systems are designed to connect, both within the system under construction and to systems that are already deployed. There are various integration mechanisms like Vertical Integration, Star Integration, Horizontal Integration or Enterprise Service Bus (ESB) and Common Data Format.

Pace IT can help you deliver cost-effective integration services that are scalable, cloud-based, innovative and result-oriented for your business. We can assist you to deal with your toughest integration challenges across your organisation making sure that your technology is in line with your business needs.

Big Data

Did you Know? The volume of data in the world is increasing at the rate of 54% per year. 90% of total data worldwide has been created in the last two years alone. By 2015, it is estimated that this volume of data will reach 7.9 Zettabytes. Unstructured data, such as text and images account for 90% of this amount.

This trend presents enormous challenges but at the same time it also presents incredible opportunities, especially under today's economic pressures where every organisation wants to do more for less. Today we have a great opportunity to take advantage of this massive amount of data i.e. Big Data, to better understand consumer markets, customer engagement, information highways, areas of potential growth or problem zones to improve operational performance.

Pace IT can help you gain powerful insight into your existing and new data to enable you to derive descriptive, predictive as well as prescriptive analytics. This will enable your organisation to gain competitive and commercial edge over your competitors. With years of experience in analytics, and commitment in developing tools that are best suited to the business needs, Pace IT can help you unlock the business value from your Big Data allowing you to make better decisions faster than ever before.

CRM Solutions

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a model for managing a company’s interactions with current and future customers. It involves using technology that can be used to measure and manage customer contacts made by phone, fax, mail, email, IM or face to face. The data that is collected can then be used for research and analysis of the customer relationship.

Pace IT can build CRM solution for you which can give you single view of your customers, focus on best prospects, stream line processes and improve business productivity. With Pace IT you can be sure that your CRM solution will be cost effective yet fully featured, robust, easy to use, scalable and maintainable.
