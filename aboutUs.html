About Us

Who We Are

Pace IT Solutions & Services is a progressive detail oriented company utilizing the deep technology expertise, domain experience and latest technologies to help deliver innovative high quality solutions to our clients that we can ultimately be proud to add our name to.

We believe in delivering solutions that are cost-effective, fast to deploy, highly scalable and reliable which can help us build long-term relationships with our clients. In order to achieve this we have built team of experts who are highly skilled, committed and passionate about what they do.

Our Mission

Pace IT Solutions & Services mission is to provide high quality solutions and services to our clients in a timely manner within a positive, progressive, rewarding, rich environment for both our employees and our clients.
We are passionate about quality, innovation and providing “value for money” solutions and services.

Our Vision

Pace IT Solutions & Services vision is to provide an optimistic environment for our customers, employees and our partners. We will work to promote positive growth and offer training that exceeds the expectations of our employees. This positive outlook for the future will be reflected in our work and our clients’ own expectations will be exceeded in every aspect of their association with us.

Our Philosophy

Pace IT Solutions & Services believes that our employees are our greatest asset. Enthusiasm and commitment are the cornerstones of our current and future success. Therefore, team members are chosen for those reasons, as well as, their technical expertise, creative thinking and innovative mind. We shall engage with our clients wholly and specifically for the sole purpose of determining their exact needs and expectations. We will then commit ourselves to exceeding those expectations.

Our work environment encourages positive growth, enabling team members to succeed. Teamwork, as well as, individuality is emphasized with the primary goal of delivering top-notch “value for money” client service. We believe that exceptional service can only be delivered by exceptional people.

We believe in the notion that every client, large or small, is our most important client and treats each one with the respect they deserve.
